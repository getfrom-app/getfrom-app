Fromly is now on the App Store: iPhone and iPad

One universal binary, the same chat you already know, now in your pocket.

Fromly Blog · July 16, 2026 · 4 min read

Fromly 2.0 is now available on the App Store, with a universal app that works equally well on iPhone and iPad. It's not a stripped-down version or a website wrapped in an app: it's the same Fromly you use in the browser or on Mac, with chat as the front door, synced in real time with the rest of your devices.

One binary, two experiences

There's no separate "iPhone app" and "iPad app" — it's the same download, the same login, the same database. What changes is how each screen adapts: on iPhone, Fromly organizes into tabs built for one-handed use (Today, Search, Chat, Settings); on iPad it uses the extra space for the canvas and side-by-side panels, closer to the web experience.

Anything you create on one device shows up instantly on the others. Start a conversation on Mac in the morning, pick it up on your iPhone on the way to a meeting, and review the canvas on your iPad that night — no exporting, no manual syncing, no thinking about it.

What you can do from your phone

  • Talk to Fromly just like on the web: create tasks, notes and events, ask about what you've already saved, attach photos and documents straight from the camera or your library.
  • Share to Fromly from any app using iOS's share sheet — an article, a screenshot, a link — it lands directly in your chat so you decide what to do with it.
  • Check today's agenda and mark tasks done without opening a computer.

Available in 12 languages from day one, with the same Profile and context Memory you already use on every other platform.

Get it

Search for "Fromly" on the App Store, or open it directly from your iPhone or iPad. If you already have a web account, just sign in — all your content is waiting for you.
