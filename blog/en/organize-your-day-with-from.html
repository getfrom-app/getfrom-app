The problem with separate apps

Most people use between 4 and 6 different apps to manage their day: one for tasks, another for notes, the phone's calendar, email, a quick scratchpad… The result is always the same: scattered information, lost context, and the constant feeling that something's slipping through the cracks.

From solves this with a radical approach: everything in a single tree. Your tasks, notes, meetings, and ideas all live in the same place, organized by date and hierarchy, accessible on web, Mac, and iPhone.

Your daily journal, always ready

Every day, From automatically creates a note with today's date. Without you having to do anything. Press H from anywhere in the app and you go straight to your daily note.

Here you can write freely — a task, an idea, a reminder, meeting notes. From understands what you write and displays it with the right format: if you write an action, it appears as a task with a checkbox; if it's a note, as hierarchical text.

💡 Daily shortcut: Press H to go to today's note, N to create a new node directly in it. You'll never start the day staring at a blank page again.

Creating tasks without breaking flow

In From, creating a task is as natural as writing. Type what you need to do and press Cmd+Enter to convert it into a task. The checkbox appears, the task is logged, and you can keep writing.

When you finish something, one click on the checkbox marks it done. The text gets a green strikethrough and the task disappears from your pending list — without being deleted, in case you need to reference it later.

  • Cmd+Enter — converts any line into a task
  • Click the checkbox — marks done / undoes
  • Tab — turns the task into a subtask of the one above
  • Shift+Tab — moves up a level

From AI: ask for what you need by voice or text

The part that surprises new users most is the built-in AI. Press Space from anywhere and From AI opens — an assistant that knows all your content and can act on it.

It's not a generic chatbot. It knows what's in your tree, what your projects are, and what's pending. You can ask it things like:

  • "Create a note 'Q3 Web Project' with tasks: design, development, and launch"
  • "Add a task for tomorrow: review sales metrics"
  • "Show me everything I have pending this week"
  • "Summarize today's meeting notes"

The AI shows you what it's going to do before executing — a confirmation card with each item it will create. Confirm with one click and your tree updates in milliseconds.

🎙 Voice mode: Hold the R key and speak. Release to send. From AI transcribes and executes your instruction without touching the keyboard.

Smart filters: find everything instantly

Over time, your tree grows. Projects, notes, tasks from different days. Finding something specific could get hard — but From includes a filter system that understands natural language.

Press Cmd+F and write what you want to see. No need to memorize technical operators: you can write "pending tasks for today" and From automatically translates it into the right filter.

You can also use direct operators for precise filters:

  • pending — only pending tasks
  • today — everything under today's note
  • pending and today — pending tasks for today
  • overdue — everything that should already be done
  • pending or tomorrow — today's and tomorrow's pending items

Your most-used filters can be saved as panels in the sidebar — one click and you see them instantly. From ships with "Pending" and "This week" built in.
