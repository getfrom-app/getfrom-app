Frequently asked questions

What happens when my subscription ends?

Your data is not deleted. Sync pauses and AI becomes unavailable, but you still access all your content on the free plan (up to 1,000 nodes). Reactivate whenever you want.

Can I switch plans?

Yes, at any time from Settings → Account → Subscription. You can switch from monthly to annual, cancel or upgrade to Lifetime without losing anything.

Does it work offline?

Mac and iPhone keep a local copy and keep working without a connection. Changes sync automatically when the network is back. The web version requires a connection.

What are AI tokens?

Tokens are the unit of consumption for language models (Claude, GPT). Every chat message, AI edit or agent run consumes tokens. If you run out, you can buy a top-up or connect your own API key.

Does the Lifetime plan include future updates?

Yes. Lifetime includes all Fromly updates forever. Pro includes updates while your subscription is active.

Can I use my own Claude or OpenAI API key?

Yes. On any Pro or Lifetime plan you can connect your Anthropic, OpenAI or Google API key in Settings → AI. The tokens you use will be charged to your provider account, not to Fromly.
